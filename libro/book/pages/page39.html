The next day, they also had breakfast in the hotel, where they met Carlos, a Mexican pilgrim who wanted to walk the Way of St James for the second time. He was much older than Sandra and Pedro, but they got along well straight away. Together they continued with the route until lunchtime.
They ate rice with fish stock and sweet potato cake for desert at Almussafes sports pub.

When they arrived in Benifaió, they went to the municipal library, where they saw the stages they wanted to walk in the following days. At the end of the day, they stayed at a nearby hotel called “Bartos”. In the evening they sat together, had a lot of fun and realized that you make good friends on long ways.
